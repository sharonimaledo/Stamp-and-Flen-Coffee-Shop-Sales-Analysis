# Project Brief: Stamp & Flen Coffee Shop Sales Analysis (2019–2022)
## Business Context

Stamp & Flen Coffee Shop is a direct-to-consumer (D2C) coffee retailer specializing in premium coffee bean products. Following a noticeable decline in financial performance beginning in 2022, the business required a detailed assessment of its historical sales data to identify the key drivers of revenue and profitability, evaluate customer purchasing behavior, and determine opportunities for business growth. This project analyzes transactional, product, and customer data to uncover sales trends, product performance, regional demand, and customer loyalty patterns that can support informed business decisions.

## Project Objective

The objective of this analysis is to evaluate the sales performance of Stamp & Flen Coffee Shop between 2019 and 2022 and translate the findings into actionable recommendations for improving profitability and supporting sustainable business growth.

Specifically, the project seeks to:

- Analyze sales and profit trends across different time periods
- Evaluate product performance by coffee type, roast type, and package size
- Assess customer loyalty and purchasing behavior across key markets
- Compare sales performance across countries to identify high-performing regions
- Generate practical recommendations to improve profitability, customer retention, and market diversification
## Analytical Scope

The analysis is based on 1,000 customer transactions recorded between 2019 and 2022, supported by 48 unique products and 1,000 customer profiles. The dataset captures transaction details, customer information, product characteristics, pricing, profitability, and purchase history.

The scope of the analysis includes:

- Sales and profitability analysis across multiple years
- Product performance analysis by coffee type, roast type, and package size
- Regional sales analysis across the United States, Ireland, and the United Kingdom
- Customer loyalty analysis to evaluate repeat purchasing behavior
- Interactive dashboard development and business-focused recommendations
## Dataset Overview

The dataset consists of three tables:

- Orders: Transaction-level sales records containing order details, quantities, pricing, sales, and profit metrics.
- Products: Product information including coffee type, roast type, package size, pricing, and profit margins.
- Customers: Customer profiles containing demographic information, location, and loyalty card status.


## Expected Business Value

This project should provide business stakeholders with:

- Clear visibility into sales and profitability trends
- Insights into high-performing products and customer purchasing patterns
- Evidence-based recommendations for improving product profitability and customer retention
- A structured business intelligence solution that supports data-driven decision-making and future performance monitoring
## Deliverable
A consolidated Excel workbook containing the dataset, methodology, Pivot Tables and KPI analysis, an interactive dashboard, business insights, and strategic recommendations.
